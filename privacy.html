Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

ARISE ("we", "our", or "the app") is a fitness accountability application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use ARISE, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, username (handle), and avatar selection when you create an account.
  • Fitness Data: Workout check-ins, workout types, streak counts, and level progression that you log in the app.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens to send you reminders (optional, with your permission).
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage patterns to improve the experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features (tracking workouts, streaks, levels)
  • To display your progress on leaderboards and Strike Teams (visible to other users)
  • To send push notifications if you enable them
  • To improve the app experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be visible to:

  • Other Users: Your handle, level, streak, rank, and Strike Team membership are visible on leaderboards and team screens.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your profile information within the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
  • Disable push notifications at any time

Children's Privacy

ARISE is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
